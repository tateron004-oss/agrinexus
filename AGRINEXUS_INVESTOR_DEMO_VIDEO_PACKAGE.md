## 30-Second Elevator Pitch

AgriNexus is an AI-enabled operating platform for rural learning, workforce development, accessible telehealth, agricultural trade, logistics, maps, and provider evidence. It is designed for communities where training, care, jobs, and market access cannot live in separate systems. The demo shows how a rural user can move from learning into work, receive accessibility-aware telehealth support, participate in agricultural commerce, and leave behind a measurable evidence trail for partners, providers, and funders.

## Full Demo Script

### 0:00 - 0:35 Opening

On screen: Login page, then sign in.

Voiceover:
AgriNexus is an AI-enabled operating platform built for rural communities where learning, workforce readiness, telehealth access, agricultural trade, logistics, and provider coordination need to work together.

This demonstration shows a functional workflow-centered product, not a static landing page. Each module creates records, updates state, and contributes to one operating picture.

Action:
Log in with the demo credentials.

### 0:35 - 1:20 Command Dashboard

On screen: Dashboard.

Voiceover:
This is the command dashboard. From one view, an operator can see learning, workforce, health, trade, map intelligence, integrations, and profile evidence.

The goal is to give partners and funders a clear view of what is happening across the community: who is learning, who is ready for work, who needs care support, what trade activity is moving, and what evidence has been created.

Action:
Click `Run WOW investor demo`.

Voiceover:
The WOW demo runs a full rural accessibility scenario across the platform. It creates learning activity, workforce readiness, telehealth support, trade movement, AI evidence, notifications, and provider events.

### 1:20 - 2:10 Learning & Development

Action:
Open `Learning`.

Voiceover:
The Learning and Development module works like a training workspace. A learner can start a course, complete lessons, take a quiz, and issue a certificate.

This matters because rural workforce programs need more than content. They need proof of progress, readiness, and credentials that can connect to jobs.

Action:
Click `Complete lesson`, confirm the workflow, then point to Learning Record and Provider Evidence.

Voiceover:
When a lesson is completed, the platform updates progress, readiness, the learning record, provider evidence, and recent activity.

Action:
Point to `Accessible Learning Mode`.

Voiceover:
For hearing-impaired and visually-impaired learners, AgriNexus includes accessibility workflows. Operators can prepare captions, transcripts, audio guidance, screen-reader outlines, large-print support, and offline packets for low-bandwidth environments.

Action:
Click `Build captions`, then `Create audio guide`.

### 2:10 - 3:00 Workforce Pipeline

Action:
Open `Workforce`.

Voiceover:
The Workforce module turns training into opportunity. It connects readiness, applications, interviews, mentor support, scheduled shifts, and earnings.

This lets a partner see whether training is producing employable candidates and where each person is in the pathway.

Action:
Click `Build profile`, `Schedule interview`, `Assign mentor`, and `Start shift`.

Voiceover:
Each action updates the candidate profile and creates a record that can be reviewed by operators, employers, workforce partners, and funders.

Action:
Scroll to Role Marketplace.

Voiceover:
The Role Marketplace shows which roles are available and what gaps still need to be closed before a learner can move into paid work.

### 3:00 - 4:10 AFAYAI Health / Telehealth

Action:
Open `AFAYAI Health`.

Voiceover:
AFAYAI Health is the supervised telehealth command center. It supports patient intake, representative connection, safety review, care planning, accessibility support, and AI-assisted guidance.

This is designed for rural settings where patients may be far from a clinic, have limited internet, need a caregiver, or require hearing and vision support.

Action:
Click `Start intake`, `Connect representative`, `Run safety review`, and `Generate care plan`.

Voiceover:
The platform does not replace a doctor. It supports the care workflow by organizing intake information, creating a care record, preparing the handoff, and keeping human review visible.

Action:
Point to `Accessible Telehealth Session`.

Voiceover:
For hearing-impaired patients, the platform can prepare caption relay, transcript support, written care guidance, and caregiver notification.

For visually-impaired patients, it can prepare audio-style guidance, screen-reader friendly summaries, large-print support, and callback or low-bandwidth fallback.

Action:
Click `Build access plan`, `Start caption relay`, and `Notify caregiver`.

Voiceover:
This is the accessibility value: support is built into the workflow before, during, and after the care interaction.

### 4:10 - 5:05 AgriTrade

Action:
Open `Agritrade`.

Voiceover:
AgriTrade supports rural economic activity. Farmers, cooperatives, buyers, and operators can manage product lots, buyer orders, wallet transactions, logistics checkpoints, and market intelligence.

Action:
Click `Create order` on a product, then `Advance order`.

Voiceover:
An order creates a trade record and moves through logistics stages. The route and checkpoint data give operators visibility into where value is moving.

Action:
Click a wallet button such as `M-Pesa +120`.

Voiceover:
The wallet area simulates mobile payment rails such as M-Pesa, MTN, and Airtel. In production, these would connect to real payment providers.

Action:
Click `Price AI` or `Route AI`.

Voiceover:
AI can assist with market pricing and route risk while creating an auditable record.

### 5:05 - 5:55 Map & AI

Action:
Open `Map & AI`.

Voiceover:
The map is operational, not decorative. It connects country context, routes, checkpoints, facilities, health pressure, logistics movement, and AI recommendations.

Action:
Click `Run command center`, `Inspect route`, and `Assess route risk`.

Voiceover:
The AI command center reviews active country, route, checkpoint, learning, workforce, health, trade, and provider context together.

Action:
Use the top selector and select `Kenya`, `DRC`, or `Egypt`.

Voiceover:
The country selector changes country context and language behavior. Kenya supports Kiswahili, DRC supports French, and Egypt supports Arabic. English remains available as its own option.

### 5:55 - 6:40 Integrations & Admin

Action:
Open `Integrations`.

Voiceover:
The Integrations module shows the provider layer. In this demo, providers are simulated for safety and speed. In production, these cards become connection points for live AI providers, databases, HR systems, telehealth systems, notification services, payment rails, logistics providers, and market data.

Action:
Click a few provider test buttons.

Voiceover:
Testing providers creates integration evidence. That is important because investors and partners need to see not only the interface, but the operating trail behind the interface.

Action:
Open `Admin`.

Voiceover:
The Admin Control Room gives operators visibility into users, module health, AI governance, notifications, audit events, and production readiness.

Action:
Click `Run health check`, then run an AI test if desired.

Voiceover:
AI activity is visible and reviewable. Human oversight stays part of the operating model, especially for health, workforce, and financial workflows.

### 6:40 - 7:20 Unified Profile & Close

Action:
Open `Profile`.

Voiceover:
The unified profile is the proof layer. Learning, workforce, health, wallet, trade, and AI activity all contribute to one operating record.

This is why AgriNexus is more than a dashboard. It is a workflow engine and evidence platform for rural transformation.

Closing voiceover:
AgriNexus is built for communities where access, training, care, and economic opportunity need to work together. The current product is a functional investor demo. The next step is production deployment, live provider credentials, field pilots, and partnerships with rural health, workforce, agriculture, and AI technology organizations.

## Investor Talking Points

- The demo is workflow-centered, not page-centered.
- Accessibility is built into learning and telehealth workflows.
- AI is supervised and auditable.
- Rural deployment can support low-bandwidth, multilingual, and field-agent workflows.
- The product can scale from local pilot to country-configured deployments.
