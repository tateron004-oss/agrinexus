Legal

Refund Policy

Subscription and service refunds should be simple, documented, and fair for rural operators, small businesses, and partner organizations.

Open platform

Subscriptions

Subscribers may request cancellation through the billing or support workflow. Access remains active through the paid billing period unless a partner agreement states otherwise.

Refund Review

Refund requests should be reviewed case by case for duplicate charges, service outages, onboarding issues, or account access problems.

Provider Costs

Third-party costs for SMS, telehealth, AI, drone, maps, payment rails, or other live providers may affect refund eligibility when those costs have already been incurred.

Support Path

Production deployments should list a support email, response window, and billing provider contact path before accepting live subscriptions.
