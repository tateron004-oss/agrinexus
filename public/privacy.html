Privacy Policy

AgriNexus is designed to keep workflow evidence visible while protecting user, patient, learner, farmer, and subscriber information.

Open platform

Data Collected

The platform may store account details, learning progress, workforce applications, telehealth intake details, trade records, map context, AI prompts, provider events, and audit logs.

Sensitive Data

Telehealth and accessibility records should be handled as sensitive information. Production deployments should use PostgreSQL, secure sessions, provider access controls, encryption in transit, and strict role permissions.

AI and Providers

When live providers are configured, relevant workflow payloads may be sent to AI, translation, communications, billing, maps, drone, workforce, health, or trade providers for the requested service.

User Control

Production operators should provide access, correction, export, retention, and deletion processes according to applicable local laws and partner requirements.
