# AgriNexus Investor Demo Video Script

Target length: 7 to 9 minutes  
Format: HD screen-recorded platform walkthrough with voiceover  
Demo URL: http://127.0.0.1:4173/  
Login: demo@agrinexus.org / Prototype2026!

## Opening Shot

On screen: AgriNexus login page.

Voiceover:
AgriNexus is an AI-enabled operating platform built to connect learning, workforce readiness, telehealth access, agriculture trade, logistics, maps, and provider evidence into one working system.

This demonstration focuses on a rural Nigeria scenario, including support for users who are hearing impaired, visually impaired, or operating in low-bandwidth environments.

Action:
Log in with the demo credentials.

## Scene 1: Command Dashboard

On screen: Dashboard after login.

Voiceover:
This is the command dashboard. From one view, an operator can see the state of learning, workforce, health, trade, AI, integrations, and profile evidence.

The goal is not to show separate landing pages. The goal is to show a platform where every module creates records, updates state, and contributes to one operational picture.

Action:
Click Run WOW investor demo.

Voiceover:
With one click, we run a full investor scenario. The platform simulates an accessible rural Nigeria workflow across learning, workforce placement, telehealth, trade logistics, AI recommendations, notifications, and provider evidence.

On screen: WOW storyboard fills in.

Voiceover:
The storyboard shows the operating sequence: an accessible learner starts, training becomes work, telehealth meets accessibility needs, AI remains supervised, trade logistics move value, and investor proof appears across the platform.

## Scene 2: Learning And Accessibility

Action:
Open Learning.

Voiceover:
The Learning and Development module works like a real training environment. A learner can start a course, complete lessons, take a quiz, and issue a certificate.

Action:
Click Complete lesson, confirm the modal.

Voiceover:
Every learning action updates the learner record, readiness, activity history, and provider evidence.

Action:
Point to Accessible Learning Mode.

Voiceover:
For users who are hearing impaired or visually impaired, the platform includes accessible learning workflows.

Action:
Click Build captions.

Voiceover:
The operator can generate captions and transcripts for a lesson.

Action:
Click Create audio guide.

Voiceover:
The operator can create audio guidance, screen-reader outlines, and large-print study support.

Action:
Click Send offline packet.

Voiceover:
For rural or low-bandwidth environments, the system can prepare an offline learning packet that supports continued progress outside a reliable internet connection.

## Scene 3: Workforce Pipeline

Action:
Open Workforce.

Voiceover:
The Workforce module turns training into opportunity. The platform connects course completion, certificates, readiness, applications, interviews, mentor support, shift scheduling, and earnings.

Action:
Click Build profile, Schedule interview, Assign mentor, and Start shift.

Voiceover:
Each step is a real workflow. It updates the candidate profile and creates evidence that can be reviewed by operators, partners, and funders.

Action:
Scroll to Role Marketplace.

Voiceover:
The Role Marketplace shows which opportunities are unlocked and which readiness gaps still need attention. This makes the path from training to paid work visible and measurable.

## Scene 4: AFAYAI Health

Action:
Open AFAYAI Health.

Voiceover:
AFAYAI Health is the supervised telehealth command center. It supports patient intake, representative escalation, safety review, and AI-assisted care planning.

Action:
Click Start intake, Connect representative, Run safety review, Generate care plan.

Voiceover:
The health workflow is designed around human oversight. The system records the intake, connects a representative, runs a safety review, and generates a care plan with provider evidence.

Action:
Point to Accessible Telehealth Session.

Voiceover:
For hearing and visually impaired patients, the system supports accessible telehealth sessions.

Action:
Click Build access plan.

Voiceover:
The access plan prepares captions, audio description, caregiver handoff, language support, and low-bandwidth fallback.

Action:
Click Start caption relay.

Voiceover:
Caption relay supports hearing-impaired users during telehealth sessions.

Action:
Click Notify caregiver.

Voiceover:
Caregiver notification supports trusted family members, community aides, or field representatives who help patients complete care workflows.

## Scene 5: AgriTrade

Action:
Open Agritrade.

Voiceover:
AgriNexus also supports rural economic activity through agriculture trade workflows.

Action:
Click Create order on a product.

Voiceover:
Operators can create buyer orders tied to products, countries, and route checkpoints.

Action:
Click Advance order.

Voiceover:
Orders move through logistics stages, creating a route timeline.

Action:
Click M-Pesa +120.

Voiceover:
Wallet activity simulates mobile payment rails such as M-Pesa, MTN, and Airtel.

Action:
Click Price AI or Route AI.

Voiceover:
AI can support market pricing and route risk review while still producing an auditable record.

## Scene 6: Map And AI

Action:
Open Map & AI.

Voiceover:
The map is not decorative. It is connected to country context, health pressure, facilities, routes, checkpoints, and AI recommendations.

Action:
Click Run command center.

Voiceover:
The command center pulls together the active country, route, checkpoint, learning, workforce, health, trade, and provider context.

Action:
Click Inspect route.

Voiceover:
The route inspector reviews field movement, provider status, and operational risk.

Action:
Click Assess route risk.

Voiceover:
Route risk can be assessed before logistics are advanced.

Action:
Change the country dropdown.

Voiceover:
Changing the country updates the map, health context, route state, and operational view.

## Scene 7: Integrations

Action:
Open Integrations.

Voiceover:
This module shows the provider layer. For the demo, provider engines are simulated. In production, these cards represent the connection points for live APIs, credentials, webhooks, databases, AI providers, payment rails, logistics systems, HR systems, and health records.

Action:
Click Test provider on a few cards.

Voiceover:
Testing a provider writes evidence into the integration event trail.

Action:
Click Test all providers.

Voiceover:
The system can exercise the full provider set and show readiness across modules.

## Scene 8: Admin And Governance

Action:
Open Admin.

Voiceover:
The Admin module gives operators visibility into users, modules, audit events, AI governance, notifications, and production readiness.

Action:
Click Run health check.

Voiceover:
The platform health check records provider and module status.

Action:
Use the AI console and click Run AI test.

Voiceover:
AI actions are not invisible. They are recorded and can be reviewed.

Action:
Click Approve latest AI.

Voiceover:
Human review is part of the operating model. This is important for healthcare, workforce, and financial workflows.

Action:
Click Notify learning, Notify workforce, Notify health, Notify trade.

Voiceover:
Notifications create additional evidence that workflows can trigger user and partner communication.

## Scene 9: Unified Profile

Action:
Open Profile.

Voiceover:
The unified profile is the proof layer. Learning, workforce, health, wallet, trade, and AI activity all contribute to one operating record.

This matters because funders and partners need to see more than a set of screens. They need to see a system of record, a workflow engine, and an evidence trail.

## Scene 10: Multilingual Demo

Action:
Use the top selector. Select English, Kenya, DRC, or Egypt.

Voiceover:
The platform supports multilingual navigation and workflow content. English is available as its own option. Kenya supports Kiswahili, DRC supports French, and Egypt supports Arabic. This is critical for cross-border deployment and for rural communities where language access can determine whether a tool is usable.

Action:
Open Dashboard, Learning, AFAYAI Health, and Map & AI after changing language.

Voiceover:
The language change affects the operating content across modules, not only the top navigation.

## Closing

On screen: Dashboard or WOW storyboard.

Voiceover:
AgriNexus is a functional investor demo of an AI-enabled operating platform for learning, workforce development, accessible telehealth, agriculture trade, logistics, maps, and provider evidence.

The current demo uses simulated provider engines for safety and speed, but the workflows, state changes, audit events, AI evidence, notifications, and integration paths are working.

The opportunity is to take this from a functional prototype into pilots with real users, live provider credentials, field partners, and production deployment.

AgriNexus is built for the communities where access, training, care, and economic opportunity need to work together.
