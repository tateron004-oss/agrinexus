# Nexus Global Platform Buildout Closeout

Phase 12 closes the global buildout train across Live Knowledge, agriculture, workforce, chronic care, provider access, communications, marketplace/logistics, activation, review/audit, offline access, and the global assistant brain.

## Completed Global Capability Areas

- Live Knowledge and citation-ready source routing.
- Agriculture, crop support, farm planning, and field visit packets.
- Training, literacy, workforce, and employer partner research packets.
- Chronic disease education for diabetes, hypertension, obesity, RPM, RTM, and community health worker support.
- Telehealth, provider bridge, pharmacy, and mobile clinic preparation packets.
- Communications preparation for email, SMS, WhatsApp, phone, and Telegram.
- Marketplace, vendor, logistics, cold storage, and route resource planning.
- Activation Center with 32 global lanes and partner onboarding/export controls.
- Review queue, audit, outcome, failed/blocked state, and export packets.
- Offline, low-bandwidth, stale-data, and Africa-first access layer.
- Global assistant brain plan with intent routing, mode dispatch, packet selection, Live Knowledge routing, follow-up state, confirmation posture, and no-execution safety.

## Active Runtime Behavior

The Standard User runtime can open the global command center, route mode cards and typed commands, prepare local packets, show credential-gated status, display Live Knowledge readiness, surface review/audit queues, and show no-execution brain plans.

## Safety Gates Preserved

The platform does not execute diagnosis, prescribing, medication changes, emergency dispatch, provider submission, telehealth launch, pharmacy/refill execution, message send, phone call, vendor contact, purchase, payment, insurance/claims, location sharing, camera, or any regulated third-party action without future verified connectors, consent, confirmation, audit, and outcome controls.
