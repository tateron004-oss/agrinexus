## Opening

On screen: Login page.

Narration:
Welcome to AgriNexus. In this training video, I will walk through each major section of the platform and show how a user can move through the connected workflows.

AgriNexus brings together learning, workforce readiness, telehealth, agricultural trade, map intelligence, integrations, administration, and profile evidence into one platform.

This video is focused on how to use the system step by step.

Action:
Log in.

## 1. Dashboard

On screen: Command Dashboard.

Narration:
The Dashboard is the main command center. It gives a quick view of the platform and helps users move into each major workflow.

Step-by-step:

1. Review the top summary cards for countries, patients, facilities, and orders.
2. Review the work queue to see active learning, workforce, health, trade, and AI tasks.
3. Use the module cards to open Learning, Workforce, Health, AgriTrade, Map & AI, or Integrations.
4. Click `Run WOW investor demo` when you want to quickly populate the platform with a complete demo scenario.
5. Review the storyboard and recent activity to see what changed.

Narration:
Use the Dashboard when you want a quick summary of what is happening across the entire platform.

## 2. Country And Language Selector

On screen: Top selector.

Narration:
At the top of the platform is the country and language selector.

Step-by-step:

1. Choose `English` to return the platform to English.
2. Choose `Nigeria` to focus the platform on Nigeria.
3. Choose `Kenya` to switch to the Kenya context and Kiswahili content.
4. Choose `DRC` to switch to the DRC context and French content.
5. Choose `Egypt` to switch to the Egypt context and Arabic content.

Narration:
This selector changes the operating context for the platform. It helps demonstrate how AgriNexus can support different countries, languages, routes, and rural deployment models.

## 3. Learning & Development

On screen: Learning section.

Narration:
The Learning and Development section is where users build skills, complete training, and earn certificates.

Step-by-step:

1. Open `Learning` from the left navigation.
2. Review the active course at the top of the page.
3. Click `Start course` to begin the learning path.
4. Click `Complete lesson` to record lesson progress.
5. Click `Complete quiz` to record assessment progress.
6. Click `Issue certificate` to create a certificate record.
7. Review the Learning Record panel to confirm readiness, hours, score, certificates, and course progress.
8. Scroll to the course catalog and select another course if needed.

Accessibility steps:

1. Find `Accessible Learning Mode`.
2. Click `Build captions` to prepare caption or transcript support.
3. Click `Create audio guide` to prepare audio or screen-reader support.
4. Click `Send offline packet` for low-bandwidth learning support.
5. Review the Learner Accommodation Plan to confirm the accessibility support was created.

Narration:
Use this section to show how learning becomes measurable. Every learning action updates progress, readiness, activity, and provider evidence.

## 4. Workforce Pipeline

On screen: Workforce section.

Narration:
The Workforce section connects training to opportunity. It helps users move from learning readiness into applications, interviews, mentor support, shifts, and role placement.

Step-by-step:

1. Open `Workforce`.
2. Review the candidate profile, readiness score, eligibility, earnings, and candidate stage.
3. Click `Build profile` to verify the candidate profile.
4. Click `Schedule interview` to create interview progress.
5. Click `Assign mentor` to add mentor support.
6. Click `Start shift` to create a scheduled work activity.
7. Review Applications and Shift Schedule to confirm the workflow created records.
8. Scroll to Role Marketplace.
9. Review which roles are open and which roles require more readiness or certificates.
10. Click an available role action when the user is ready to apply.

Narration:
Use this section to show that AgriNexus does not stop at training. It helps users move toward real work and creates evidence along the way.

## 5. AFAYAI Health / Telehealth

On screen: AFAYAI Health section.

Narration:
AFAYAI Health is the telehealth and care coordination workspace. It is designed for rural access, patient intake, accessibility support, and supervised care workflows.

Step-by-step:

1. Open `AFAYAI Health`.
2. Review the Active Case panel.
3. Click `Start intake` to create a patient intake.
4. Click `Connect representative` to simulate a care representative handoff.
5. Click `Run safety review` to record a safety review.
6. Click `Generate care plan` to create care-plan guidance.
7. Review Intake Queue, Care Plans, Safety Reviews, and AI Governance.

Accessibility steps:

1. Find `Accessible Telehealth Session`.
2. Click `Build access plan` to prepare caption, audio, caregiver, language, and low-bandwidth support.
3. Click `Start caption relay` for hearing-impaired users.
4. Click `Notify caregiver` to create a caregiver or support-person handoff.
5. Review Accessibility Case Notes to confirm the support was recorded.

Narration:
Use this section to explain that AgriNexus does not replace doctors. It helps organize the care journey, prepare information, support accessibility, and keep human oversight visible.

## 6. AgriTrade

On screen: AgriTrade section.

Narration:
AgriTrade is where agricultural products, buyer orders, wallet activity, and logistics movement come together.

Step-by-step:

1. Open `Agritrade`.
2. Review the product cards.
3. Click `Create order` on a product to create a buyer order.
4. Review Order Operations and Order Book.
5. Click `Advance order` to move the order through logistics stages.
6. Click a wallet payment button such as `M-Pesa +120`, `MTN +140`, or `Airtel +110`.
7. Review Wallet Ledger to confirm the transaction.
8. Click `Price AI` to review pricing guidance.
9. Click `Route AI` to review logistics or route guidance.
10. Review Trade Events and Trade AI Evidence.

Narration:
Use this section to show how AgriNexus can help farmers and cooperatives organize sales, orders, payments, and logistics.

## 7. Map & AI

On screen: Map & AI section.

Narration:
The Map & AI section gives operators a geographic view of country context, routes, checkpoints, facilities, and AI recommendations.

Step-by-step:

1. Open `Map & AI`.
2. Review the map and active country context.
3. Review Map Inspector for country, route, checkpoint, risk, queue, and route stage.
4. Click `Run command center` to run a broad AI review.
5. Click `Inspect route` to review route movement and operating context.
6. Click `Assess route risk` to create route risk guidance.
7. Review Layer Status, Route Intelligence, and AI Run History.
8. Use the top country selector to change country context and watch the map and operating data update.

Narration:
Use this section to show that the map is operational. It is connected to health, logistics, country context, routes, and AI activity.

## 8. Integrations

On screen: Integrations section.

Narration:
The Integrations section shows the provider layer. In the demo, these providers are simulated. In production, they represent real connection points to outside systems.

Step-by-step:

1. Open `Integrations`.
2. Review provider cards for learning, workforce, health, trade, AI, maps, and persistence.
3. Click `Test provider` on individual cards.
4. Review Integration Events to confirm provider activity.
5. Review Module Activation and Live Connection Checklist.
6. Review Environment Readiness to see what is connected, ready, or still needs setup.

Narration:
Use this section to show that AgriNexus is designed to connect with real providers, including AI, databases, health systems, HR systems, payment tools, logistics providers, and notification services.

## 9. Admin Control Room

On screen: Admin section.

Narration:
The Admin Control Room helps operators manage readiness, governance, users, module health, AI review, notifications, and audit activity.

Step-by-step:

1. Open `Admin`.
2. Click `Run health check`.
3. Review AI Integration Console.
4. Select an AI workflow from the test dropdown.
5. Click `Run AI test`.
6. Review AI Evidence.
7. Use `Approve latest AI` or `Reject latest AI` to demonstrate human review.
8. Use notification buttons to notify Learning, Workforce, Health, or Trade.
9. Review Users, Module Health, Audit Feed, Production Readiness, and Next Setup Steps.

Narration:
Use this section to show that AgriNexus includes governance and oversight. This is important for healthcare, workforce, AI, and funding conversations.

## 10. Unified Profile

On screen: Profile section.

Narration:
The Unified Profile is the proof layer. It brings together the user’s progress across learning, workforce, health, wallet, trade, orders, and AI activity.

Step-by-step:

1. Open `Profile`.
2. Review Learning progress.
3. Review Workforce readiness.
4. Review Health activity.
5. Review Wallet and Orders.
6. Review AI activity.

Narration:
Use this section at the end of a demo to show that actions across the platform are not isolated. They roll into one operating record that partners, funders, and program teams can understand.

## Suggested Training Close

On screen: Return to Dashboard or Profile.

Narration:
That completes the AgriNexus platform walkthrough.

The main idea is that each section is connected. Learning can lead to workforce readiness. Workforce activity can connect to opportunity. Health workflows can support rural care and accessibility. AgriTrade can help farmers and cooperatives organize market activity. Map and AI can help operators understand what is happening by location. Integrations and Admin provide the evidence, oversight, and readiness needed for real deployment.

AgriNexus is designed to help rural communities learn, work, access care, trade, and grow through one connected platform.
